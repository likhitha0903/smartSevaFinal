SmartSeva

Transforming citizen grievances into responsive, real-time governance.

SmartSeva is a digital platform that simplifies how citizens report civic issues and connects them directly with the concerned departments. It ensures that problems like potholes, water leakage, waste overflow, and safety hazards are handled in a clear and timely manner.

Our Mission: To deliver a fast, transparent, and accountable grievance system that provides timely updates and dependable service.

SmartSeva uses AI technologies such as grievance understanding, image severity detection, and smart routing to make sure every issue reaches the right team without delay.

Citizens receive live status updates, easy escalation options, and an accessible mobile-first interface—making public service delivery more efficient and citizen-friendly.

Major Issues Faced by Citizens

SmartSeva identifies and addresses the most pressing civic challenges affecting daily life.

Overflowing Garbage

Waste accumulation causes foul smell, mosquito breeding & serious health hazards in residential areas.

Potholes & Damaged Roads

Damaged road surfaces are a common cause of accidents and major traffic slowdowns during peak hours.

Water Leakage

Leaking pipes lead to significant water wastage and can weaken road structures over time.

Power Cuts

Frequent electricity disruptions affect households, students' education and local businesses.

Open / Blocked Drains

Clogged drainage systems cause foul smell, mosquito breeding & flooding during rains.

Child Labour

Children forced into labour lose educational opportunities and face long-term developmental issues.

Non-Functional Street Lights

Dark streets and neighborhoods increase risks of crime, accidents, and reduce community safety.

Dirty Public Toilets

Poorly maintained public facilities cause hygiene issues and public health concerns.

Contaminated Water

Polluted drinking water sources lead to waterborne diseases and serious health complications.

Encroached Footpaths

Blocked footpaths force pedestrians onto busy roads, increasing chances of accidents.

Waterlogging

Stagnant water from poor drainage causes traffic jams, mosquito breeding & inconvenience.

Noise Pollution

Excessive honking & construction noise disturb daily life and impact mental well-being.

Burning Waste

Open waste burning releases toxic smoke causing pollution & severe health risks.

Loose Electrical Wires

Exposed wires pose high chances of electrocution, fires & public safety hazards.

Stray Animals

Uncontrolled stray animals create fear, road accidents & sanitation issues in public areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about filing grievance, tracking, AI prioritization, privacy and more.

Sign in (or continue as guest if enabled), choose the correct department, describe the issue, attach photos or video, and submit. You’ll receive a unique token to track the grievance.

Include exact location (landmark/street), a short clear description, date/time, and photos or short videos showing the problem. Evidence speeds up verification and action.

Our models analyse the grievance text and attached images to detect category, severity, hazards (e.g., exposed wires, deep potholes), and sentiment (urgent vs routine). The system then suggests a priority so officers can address the most critical issues first.

Time-to-resolution varies by department and priority. After submission you can track the grievancestatus using your token. High priority/urgent issues are routed to immediate action where possible; follow-up timelines appear in the grievance details.

You can file grievance as a registered user for better tracking and history. Some jurisdictions may also allow guest submissions — check the form. Registered users get notifications and a history of previous grievance.

Use the grievance page to request escalation or add comments. If unresolved, follow the escalation path shown in grievance details (escalation to senior officer / grievance cell). You may also contact support via the helpline listed in the footer.

SmartSeva follows data protection practices: personal data is used only for grievance processing and communication. Uploaded media is stored securely and shared only with relevant departments. For details see the Privacy policy link in the footer.

We accept common image formats (JPEG/PNG) and short videos (MP4/WebM). Recommended max file size is listed on the upload form — compress large files before upload. Avoid uploading sensitive personal information in images.

For technical issues or accessibility assistance, email smartsevaforpeople@gmail.com or call the helpline number listed in the footer. Provide the token or screenshot to help us troubleshoot faster.
