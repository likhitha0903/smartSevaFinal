Road Damage & Potholes

Potholes cause vehicle damage and accidents — reporting quickly reduces risk.

Overview

Road damage includes potholes, sinkholes, and surface wear — these affect safety and mobility.

Consequences of Neglect

  • Vehicle damage & repair costs for residents
  • Higher accident risk, especially at night
  • Traffic congestion and longer commute times
  • Higher emergency response times for ambulances

What You Can Do

  • Report exact location with photo and severity
  • Avoid the area and warn neighbours until repaired

How SevaSetu Helps

AI flags high-risk road damage and escalates urgent repairs to the Roads Dept with SLAs and progress tracking.
