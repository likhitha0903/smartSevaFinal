About SmartSeva

SmartSeva is a modern grievance support platform designed to simplify the way citizens report civic issues. From potholes and road damage to waste management and child safety concerns, the system allows users to share problems through a quick photo upload and brief description.

The platform uses intelligent analysis to understand the nature of the grievance and guide it to the right department for timely action. Citizens receive clarity and transparency throughout the process, with the ability to follow the status of their issue at every stage.

At its core, SmartSeva strives to improve public services by enabling faster communication, encouraging accountability, and empowering communities to participate in building a cleaner and safer environment.

Grievance Path

Escalation Ladder

1
Field / Ward Officer
First responder for verification and local action. Most routine grievance are resolved at this level.
2
Zonal / Circle Officer
Handles escalated or repeated issues, coordinates with multiple departments if required.
3
Grievance Cell
Reviews SLA breaches, citizen feedback and unresolved grievance, and may initiate disciplinary action.
Escalate from SmartSeva
Use the “Request Escalation” option if your grievance remains unresolved after the indicated timeline.
